owner: Array(Nf), index of the cell on the "left" of the Face
neighbor: Array(Nf), index of the other one. If boundary: -1 -> look at physics tag

Sf: array(Nf,2), vector normal to the Face
magDf: array(Nf), distance between owner and neighbour
Cf: array(Nf,2)coordinates of face center

boundary tags: (array,Nf): internal face(-1), wall(0),inlet(1),outlet(2)
